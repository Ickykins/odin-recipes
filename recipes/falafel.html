Chrispy Falafel

Description

Credit to Madelain Farah’s “Lebanese Cuisine” for this recipe and many of my other favorite Lebanese recipes. This makes for a delicioius and flavorful Falafel, that combined with other dips and pickles makes a mouth wateringly good sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry uncooked chickpeas
  • 1 small onion, coarsly chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 t. ground coriander
  • 1/4 t. hot (red) pepper, optional (but not really)
  • 1 t. soda
  • 1/2 t. cumin
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 T. flour
  • 1/2 c. oil

Instructions

  1. Soak beans in water over night.
  2. After soaking place beans in a food processor with the onion. Add the rest of the ingredients EXCEPT THE OIL and mix well. Process the mixture a second time.

Optional

  • Falafel is delicious in a sandwich with syrian bread, but if you're stuck without (because your local store is closed or out, or you were too lazy to pick it up) our pizza dough recipe made in the Optional leftover dough sandwich configuration works perfectly and has a delicious crunch and chew. Beware though, that’s a lot of sandwich.

  • Falafel tastes good with all or some of the following;

    • Tahini sauce
    • Tabouli
    • Hummus
    • Baba Ganoush
    • Tatziki
    • Pickles
